Mission Statement

SkillsGuild offers a platform that fosters a culture, learning, and social environment. Our team created this Android application for holders of a unique skill to share their knowledge with others and to find a place to learn unique skills that are otherwise difficult to find training for. Users can pay to access more advanced class management features. Users who are only using the application to take classes do not have a charge to pay and so can use the application free of charge.
